About Green Pulse Education

Dedicated to empowering the next generation of environmental stewards

Our Mission

Our mission at Green Pulse Education is to serve as a non-profit environmental educational organization dedicated to K-12 communities within the greater Chicago area and beyond. We aim to educate and motivate students, empowering them to protect the environment through tactical and strategic engagement. We guide students in transforming their environmental ideas into actionable plans that yield measurable, sustainable, and far-reaching results.

Our History

2018

Foundation

Green Pulse Education was founded by a group of educators and environmental scientists who recognized the urgent need to connect students directly with environmental challenges in their communities.

2019

First Programs Launch

Started with just 5 schools in the Chicago area, launching our flagship Green Schools Initiative and Water Stewardship programs.

2021

Rapid Expansion

Expanded to serve over 50 schools across Illinois, introducing innovative programs like the Zero Waste Challenge and Urban Forest Initiative.

2024

National Recognition

Now serving over 120 educational institutions across multiple states, recognized as a leading voice in environmental education nationwide.
