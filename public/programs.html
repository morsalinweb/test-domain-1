Our Programs

Comprehensive environmental education that creates lasting change

Transforming Education Through Action

Our programs go beyond traditional classroom learning, empowering students to become environmental leaders through hands-on projects that create real, measurable impact in their communities.

Flagship Program

Green Schools Initiative

Our flagship program transforms schools into living laboratories for sustainability. Students conduct comprehensive environmental audits and implement solutions that reduce their school's environmental footprint while learning valuable STEM skills.

Energy efficiency audits and improvements
Waste reduction and recycling programs
School garden and composting systems
Student-led sustainability committees
60 Schools Participating
30% Average Energy Reduction
8,500 Students Involved
Science Focus

Water Stewardship Program

Students become water quality scientists, monitoring local waterways and implementing conservation projects. This hands-on program covers watershed protection, pollution prevention, and sustainable water management practices.

Water quality testing and monitoring
Watershed mapping and protection
Storm drain marking and education
Rain garden installation
25 Monitoring Sites
3,200 Student Scientists
12 Community Projects
Action Oriented

Zero Waste Challenge

Teaching circular economy principles through comprehensive waste reduction strategies. Students design recycling programs, composting systems, and work to eliminate single-use plastics from their schools and communities.

Waste audits and reduction planning
Comprehensive recycling systems
Composting and organic waste management
Plastic-free initiatives
40 Schools at 75%+ Diversion
2.3M Pounds Diverted
5,800 Students Participating
Ecosystem Focus

Urban Forest Initiative

Students learn about urban ecology while actively improving their communities through tree planting, habitat restoration, and biodiversity conservation projects. This program addresses air quality, urban heat islands, and wildlife habitat.

Tree planting and care programs
Native habitat restoration
Biodiversity monitoring
Urban heat island mitigation
1,200 Trees Planted
15 Restoration Sites
2,800 Students Involved
