The Climate Crisis: By the Numbers

Real data showing why environmental education matters now more than ever.

Global Temperature Rise
+1.1°C
Since pre-industrial times (1880-2023). The last decade includes 9 of the 10 warmest years on record.
CO₂ Atmospheric Levels
421 ppm
Highest in 3+ million years. Pre-industrial levels were 280 ppm. Current rate of increase: 2.4 ppm/year.
Sea Level Rise
21.6 cm
Since 1880, with acceleration to 3.4 mm/year since 2006. Projected 0.43-2.84m rise by 2100.
Arctic Sea Ice Loss
13% per decade
Decline in summer sea ice extent. 2023 saw the 6th lowest minimum extent on record.
