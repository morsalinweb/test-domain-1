Success Stories

Real projects with real impact, led by students in our programs

Award Winner

Lincoln High School Solar Project

Students at Lincoln High designed and installed a 50kW solar array, reducing the school's energy costs by 35% annually while creating a living laboratory for renewable energy education.

$8,500 annual savings
68 tons CO₂ reduced yearly
45 students involved

"This project showed our students that they can be part of the solution to climate change."

- Sarah Johnson, Science Teacher
Community Impact

Roosevelt Middle School Water Quality

8th graders discovered elevated nitrate levels in a local stream, leading to identification of pollution sources and successful advocacy for improved water treatment.

40% water quality improvement
City policy changes implemented
28 students as water scientists

"Our students became real environmental advocates and made a difference in our community."

- Michael Chen, Principal
Zero Waste Champion

Washington Elementary Zero Waste

Students implemented a comprehensive waste reduction program, achieving 85% waste diversion and saving the school $3,200 annually in waste management costs.

85% waste diversion rate
$3,200 annual savings
2 tons of compost produced

"The kids are so proud of what they've accomplished. They're teaching their families too!"

- Lisa Rodriguez, Custodial Manager
