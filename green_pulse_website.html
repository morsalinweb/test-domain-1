About Green Pulse Education

Dedicated to empowering the next generation of environmental stewards

Our Mission

Our mission at Green Pulse Education is to serve as a non-profit environmental educational organization dedicated to K-12 communities within the greater Chicago area and beyond. We aim to educate and motivate students, empowering them to protect the environment through tactical and strategic engagement. We guide students in transforming their environmental ideas into actionable plans that yield measurable, sustainable, and far-reaching results.

Our History

Founded in 2018, Green Pulse Education emerged from a recognition that environmental education needed to go beyond textbook learning. Our founders, a group of educators and environmental scientists, saw the urgent need to connect students directly with environmental challenges in their communities.

Starting with just 5 schools in the Chicago area, we've grown to serve over 120 educational institutions across Illinois and neighboring states. Our approach combines rigorous environmental science education with practical, actionable projects that create real change.

Today, we're proud to be recognized as a leading voice in environmental education, with our programs serving as models for other organizations nationwide.
